# Invoices

View and download invoices for your Organization from the Admin Panel.

Requires the **Admin** or **Billing** role.

<SectionTab as="h1" sectionId="access">Access your invoices</SectionTab>

1. Open <AppLink href="https://admin.mistral.ai/organization/billing" app="admin" path={["Subscriptions", "Billing"]} />.
2. Scroll to the **Invoices** section.
3. Click an invoice to download it as a PDF.

<SectionTab as="h1" sectionId="contents">What invoices include</SectionTab>

Each invoice can include:

- **Subscription charges**: fixed fees for Vibe Work plans (Pro, Team, Enterprise).
- **API usage charges**: pay-as-you-go consumption billed at the end of each billing period.
- **Seat allocations**: per-seat charges for Team or Enterprise plan members.

<SectionTab as="h1" sectionId="payment-schedule">Payment schedule</SectionTab>

- **Vibe subscriptions** (Pro, Team, Enterprise): billed at the start of each billing period (monthly or annual).
- **API usage** (Scale plan): billed based on token consumption at the end of each billing period. There is no upfront cost.
- **Plan upgrades**: when upgrading a Vibe subscription, payment is due immediately for the new plan.

<SectionTab as="h1" sectionId="vat">VAT and billing details</SectionTab>

Enter your VAT number and billing address in <AppLink href="https://admin.mistral.ai/organization/billing" app="admin" path={["Subscriptions", "Billing"]} />. These details appear on all future invoices. Contact [support](https://help.mistral.ai) to update details on a past invoice.
