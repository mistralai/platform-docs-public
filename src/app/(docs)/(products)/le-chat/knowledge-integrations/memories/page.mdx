# Memories

Memories lets Le Chat **remember key details about you**, such as your preferences, interests, and working context, based on your past interactions.

Instead of repeating yourself every conversation, Memories keeps that information available so responses stay relevant and personalized.

<SectionTab as="h1" sectionId="how-it-works">How it works</SectionTab>

Le Chat can save details in two ways:

- **Explicit saving**: ask Le Chat to save something specific. For example, *"Remember that I prefer concise answers"* or *"Remember that I work with TypeScript."*
- **Automatic saving**: Le Chat may also pick up useful details from your conversations on its own. If you mention recurring preferences or context, it might store them to improve future interactions.

Saved Memories are recalled automatically when they're relevant to a new conversation.

:::info
Memories only stores information you've explicitly included in your prompts. It won't infer details you haven't communicated. Le Chat is also designed not to remember sensitive data such as social security numbers or banking information.
:::

<SectionTab as="h1" sectionId="enabling-memories">Enabling Memories</SectionTab>

You can turn on Memories in two ways:

1. Click the `Enable Memories` button in the pop-up that appears the first time you log in.
2. Open the `Memories` tab under the `Intelligence` menu on the left-hand side of your screen. Click the three dots `...` and toggle `Memories in Le Chat` on.

You'll see a confirmation message once Memories is active.

<SectionTab as="h1" sectionId="managing-memories">Managing your Memories</SectionTab>

All your Memories appear in the `Memories` tab, listed from newest to oldest. You have full control over what's stored:

- **View**: open the `Memories` tab to see everything Le Chat has saved.
- **Edit**: click the pencil icon next to a memory, make your changes in the popup, and confirm.
- **Delete one**: click the trash icon next to a memory and confirm the deletion.
- **Delete all**: click the three dots `...` at the top-right of the `Memories` tab, select `Clear all Memories`, and confirm. This action is irreversible.

You can also ask Le Chat to remove specific Memories in conversation. For example, *"Forget that I mentioned my location."*

<SectionTab as="h1" sectionId="turning-off-memories">Turning off Memories</SectionTab>

Open the `Memories` tab and toggle the Memories button off.

:::caution
Turning off Memories doesn't automatically delete your stored Memories. If you want to remove them, click `Clear all Memories` before deactivating.
:::

<SectionTab as="h1" sectionId="privacy">Privacy and data</SectionTab>

- Memories are private to your account. They're never shared with or sold to anyone, and we use them solely to improve your experience.
- Memories are stored for the duration of your subscription.
- **Free, Pro, and Student plans**: prompts that include Memories may be used to train our models. You can [opt out](https://help.mistral.ai/en/articles/455207-can-i-opt-out-of-my-input-or-output-data-being-used-for-training) in your account settings.
- **Team and Enterprise plans**: opted out of model training by default.
- We don't use Memories data for advertising, ad profiles, or data selling.
