# Observability

Observability helps you understand what your LLM applications are doing in production, measure response quality at scale, and iterate with confidence.

:::info
The entire Observability suite (Explorer, Judges, Campaigns, and Datasets) is available to **Enterprise-tier organizations** only.
:::

<SectionTab as="h1" sectionId="what-observability-does">What Observability does</SectionTab>

The Observability suite gives you three core capabilities:

- **Visibility**: see what's happening in your production traffic, event by event.
- **Quality signals**: score and classify assistant responses automatically with LLM-powered [Judges](./observability/judges).
- **Iteration loops**: use [Campaigns](./observability/campaigns) to annotate traffic at scale and build quality-tagged [Datasets](./observability/datasets).

<SectionTab as="h1" sectionId="the-four-components">The four components</SectionTab>

These capabilities are built around four components that work together.

<ExplorerTabs id="concepts">
  <ExplorerTab value="explorer" label="Explorer">

Explorer lets you **search**, **filter**, and **inspect every chat completion event** flowing through your workspace.

You can **explore individual conversations** (including messages, tool calls, and metadata) and **export filtered slices** to [Datasets](./observability/datasets) for deeper analysis.

<Badge variant="outline" size="sm" className="uppercase font-mono mb-1">When to use it?</Badge> *You want to understand what's happening in production, investigate a quality issue, or find representative examples for later analysis.*

[Go to Explorer →](./observability/explorer)

  </ExplorerTab>
<ExplorerTab value="judges" label="Judges">

Judges are Agents that **grade chat completion events** based on criteria you define. 

Two types of Judges are available:
- **Classification:** Outputs discrete labels (e.g., pass/fail, safe/unsafe, topic categories).
- **Regression:** Outputs a numeric score within a defined range (e.g., a 1–5 helpfulness rating).

<Badge variant="outline" size="sm" className="uppercase font-mono mb-1">When to use it?</Badge> *You need to programmatically evaluate model outputs across large volumes of traffic.*

:::note
Judges are not run directly: **attach them to** [Campaigns](./observability/campaigns) to score live traffic.
:::

[Go to Judges →](./observability/judges)

  </ExplorerTab>
  <ExplorerTab value="campaigns" label="Campaigns">

Campaigns uses [Judges](./observability/judges) to **evaluate your production traffic** at scale.

Annotations are saved to [Explorer](./observability/explorer), allowing you to **filter and export conversations** by quality label later.

<Badge variant="outline" size="sm" className="uppercase font-mono mb-1">When to use it?</Badge> *You want to score a batch of production traffic to find problems or build quality-tagged datasets.*

[Go to Campaigns →](./observability/campaigns)

  </ExplorerTab>
  <ExplorerTab value="datasets" label="Datasets">

Datasets are **collections of conversation records** (including messages, properties, and metadata).

Build them from [Explorer](./observability/explorer) searches, [Campaign](./observability/campaigns) results, or import your own data via JSONL files.

<Badge variant="outline" size="sm" className="uppercase font-mono mb-1">When to use it?</Badge> *Use Datasets to curate, edit, and reuse conversation records for analysis or Judge evaluation.*

:::tip
Datasets are **fully editable**: add expected outputs, fix messages, and remove noise directly in [Studio](https://console.mistral.ai/observability/datasets).
:::

[Go to Datasets →](./observability/datasets)

  </ExplorerTab>
</ExplorerTabs>
