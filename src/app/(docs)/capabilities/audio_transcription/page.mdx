# Audio transcription

Convert **speech to text** (STT) with high accuracy and low latency. We offer two models for different use cases: [Offline transcription](audio_transcription/offline_transcription) and [Realtime transcription](audio_transcription/realtime_transcription).

:::info
Both models support **GDPR and HIPAA-compliant deployments** through on-premise or private cloud setups. [Get in touch with the team](https://mistral.ai/contact) to learn more.
:::


<SectionTab as="h1" sectionId="offline-transcription">Offline transcription</SectionTab>

[Voxtral Mini Transcribe 2](../models/voxtral-mini-transcribe-26-02) (`voxtral-mini-2602`) is an efficient audio model optimized for offline transcription:

- **High accuracy**: Low word error rates across languages.
- **Speaker diarization**: Automatically identifies and labels different speakers.
- **Context biasing**: Guide the model with custom vocabulary for domain-specific terms.
- **Word-level timestamps**: Precise timestamps for each word, useful for subtitles and audio search.
- **Multilingual**: Supports 13 languages including English, Chinese, Hindi, Spanish, Arabic, French, Portuguese, Russian, German, Japanese, Korean, Italian, and Dutch.
- **Noise robustness**: Maintains accuracy in challenging acoustic environments.
- **Long audio**: Processes recordings up to three hours in a single request.

<SectionTab as="h1" sectionId="realtime-transcription">Realtime transcription</SectionTab>

[Voxtral Mini Transcribe Realtime](../models/voxtral-mini-transcribe-realtime-26-02) (`voxtral-mini-transcribe-realtime-2602`) is an efficient audio model optimized for live transcription:

- **Low latency**: Configurable down to sub-200ms, ideal for voice agents and real-time interactions.
- **Streaming**: Transcribes audio as it arrives for natural, responsive voice interactions.
- **Multilingual**: Strong performance in 13 languages.
- **Edge deployment**: Deploy on edge devices with a 4B parameter footprint for privacy-first applications.
- **Open weights**: Available under the Apache 2.0 license on [Hugging Face](https://huggingface.co/mistralai/Voxtral-Mini-4B-Realtime-2602).
