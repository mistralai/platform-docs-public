# Subscriptions

Manage your subscription plan from the Admin Panel. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel at any time.

<SectionTab as="h1" sectionId="plans">Available plans</SectionTab>

We offer several plans to match different needs:

- **Free**: core features with daily usage limits.
- **Pro**: higher usage limits for individual power users.
- **Student**: Pro features at a reduced rate for students and educators.
- **Team**: Pro features plus role-based access, centralized billing, and enterprise Connectors.
- **Enterprise**: Team features plus SAML SSO, audit logs, custom deployment, and SLA support.

Annual billing is available for Pro and Team plans at a discounted rate compared to monthly.

For current pricing, see the [pricing page](https://mistral.ai/pricing).

<SectionTab as="h1" sectionId="upgrading">Upgrade your plan</SectionTab>

1. <AppLink href="https://admin.mistral.ai/subscriptions" app="admin">Subscriptions</AppLink>.
2. Click **Compare plans** to view options.
3. Select your plan and billing frequency (monthly or annual).
4. Enter billing information (individual or company, VAT number if applicable).
5. Confirm payment.

You can upgrade at any time: from Free to Pro/Team, or from Pro to Team. Payment is due immediately and at the start of each billing period.

Accepted payment methods: credit card and Google Pay.

<SectionTab as="h1" sectionId="canceling">Cancel your subscription</SectionTab>

1. <AppLink href="https://admin.mistral.ai/subscriptions" app="admin">Subscriptions</AppLink>.
2. Click **Unsubscribe** in the Downgrade section.
3. You'll receive a confirmation email.

You keep full access until the end of the current billing period, then your account reverts to the Free plan. Before the period ends, you can click **Resume subscription** to restore your plan.

<SectionTab as="h1" sectionId="refunds">Refund policy</SectionTab>

Refunds are available only within **14 days of the initial purchase**. Contact [support](https://help.mistral.ai) to request a refund within this window. No refunds apply after 14 days.
