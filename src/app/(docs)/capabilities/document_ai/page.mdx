# Document AI

Document AI turns documents into **structured, machine-readable data**. It combines **optical character recognition** (OCR) with data extraction to handle PDFs, images, and office files in 13 languages across complex layouts.

Feed it a scanned invoice, a multi-column report, or a deck of slides, and get back clean text, tables, and metadata you can use directly in your application.

<SectionTab as="h1" sectionId="get-started">Get started</SectionTab>

Call `client.ocr.process` in our SDKs or the `https://api.mistral.ai/v1/ocr` endpoint.

Here's what you can build:

- [Extract text with OCR](document_ai/basic_ocr): Pull text, tables, and structure from documents and images.
- [Annotate and extract data](document_ai/annotations): Get structured fields from your documents using built-in Structured Outputs.
- [Ask questions about documents](document_ai/document_qna): Combine our models with OCR for document question answering.
