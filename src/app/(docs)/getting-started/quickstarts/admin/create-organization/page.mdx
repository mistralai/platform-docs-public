# Create your organization

Set up a Mistral organization from a new account through <AppLink href="https://admin.mistral.ai">Admin</AppLink>.

- Activate billing so API keys work
- Create your first workspace
- Invite team members and assign roles

Once complete, your team has working API keys and access to the platform.

**Time to complete:** ~15 minutes

<SectionTab as="h2" sectionId="prerequisites">Prerequisites</SectionTab>

- A Mistral AI account. <AppLink href="https://console.mistral.ai">Create account</AppLink>
- A credit card or cloud billing account (AWS or Azure Marketplace).
- The email address of at least one colleague to invite.

<SectionTab as="h2" sectionId="step-1">Step 1: Create your organization</SectionTab>

Log in to <AppLink href="https://console.mistral.ai">Studio</AppLink>. New accounts are prompted to name their organization and redirected to Admin automatically.

Already have an account without an organization? Open <AppLink href="https://admin.mistral.ai">Admin</AppLink> and follow the setup prompt.

<SectionTab as="h2" sectionId="step-2">Step 2: Activate billing</SectionTab>

Team members can't generate working API keys until billing is active.

Open <AppLink href="https://admin.mistral.ai/organization/billing" app="admin">Subscriptions › Billing</AppLink>, click **Add payment method**, and connect your card or cloud marketplace account. Optionally set a **Monthly spending limit** before saving.

Billing takes 2–3 minutes to take effect. If team members report inactive API keys right after, ask them to wait and retry.

<SectionTab as="h2" sectionId="step-3">Step 3: Create a workspace</SectionTab>

Your default workspace is created automatically: skip this step if one workspace is enough.

Open <AppLink href="https://admin.mistral.ai/organization/workspaces" app="admin">Manage › Workspaces</AppLink>, click **Create workspace**, and give it a name (for example, "Engineering" or "Production").

<SectionTab as="h2" sectionId="step-4">Step 4: Invite team members</SectionTab>

Open <AppLink href="https://admin.mistral.ai/organization/members" app="admin">Administration › Members</AppLink>, click **Invite member**, enter the email address, and assign a role:

<SectionTab as="h2" sectionId="step-5">Step 5: Security and monitoring</SectionTab>

As your team grows, control who can access what.

**Configure SSO:**
Enforce enterprise authentication with SAML-based SSO. Navigate to **Administration › Settings** in the Admin panel to configure your identity provider and define an **Allowed email domain** to block invites to personal or external addresses.

**Monitor audit logs:**
Open <AppLink href="https://admin.mistral.ai/audit-logs" app="admin">Administration › Audit logs</AppLink> to track critical events. This gives you a record of when API keys were created, who was invited, and when billing settings changed.

<SectionTab as="h2" sectionId="verify">Verify</SectionTab>

The invited team member receives an email within a few minutes. Once they accept, their status in <AppLink href="https://admin.mistral.ai/organization/members" app="admin">Administration › Members</AppLink> changes from **Pending** to **Active**.
